The Story of Bellaviva Skin Clinic

Sunita Sharma, the founder of Bellaviva Skin Clinic, has over 25 years of experience in the Skin Care Industry. Being Consultant for high end skin care companies e.g: Clinic, La-Mer and Christian Dior Company as a Senior Skin Care Consultant. She treated various ethnicities and their different types of skin and conditions. She has worked with plastic surgeons as a para-medical aesthetician in Hawaii. Sunita strongly believes that healthy mind, body and skin is the key to confidence.

At Bellaviva Skin Clinic, we as professional skin experts team do one-to-one personal skin consultation and tailor their treatment plan according to their customer's needs and concerns. As we understand that all customers are individuals, we strive to offer high quality, tailored treatments to ensure our clients to achieve to look and feel their best in their skin.

Bellaviva Skin Clinic is designed to be comfortable and spacious where we assure our clients are welcome and relaxed during their treatment time with us.
